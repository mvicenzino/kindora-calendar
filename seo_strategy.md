# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages
- Public informational pages (`/`, `/why`, `/about`, `/help`, `/support`, `/resources`)
- Public legal and trust pages (`/terms`, `/privacy`)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated application routes (`/calendar`, `/tasks`, `/chores`, `/meals`, `/care`, `/messages`, `/documents`, `/memories`, `/advisor`, `/health`, `/settings`, `/admin`)
- Auth recovery and verification flows unless they create a broader crawlability issue (`/forgot-password`, `/reset-password`, `/verify-email`)
- Tokenized emergency bridge routes intended for private sharing (`/emergency-bridge/:token`)

## Target audience
- Families, caregivers, and adults managing care for children and/or aging parents.

- family calendar
- caregiver calendar
- family scheduling app
- sandwich generation app
- eldercare coordination
- caregiving organizer
